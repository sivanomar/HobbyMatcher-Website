About Us

HobbyMatcher was created to help people of all ages find other keen hobbyists with similar interests and hobbies. From knitting to golfing, whatever your hobby may be, this site will help you find people who share your interest.

Register with HobbyMatcher today to get started!
It only takes 3 simple steps.

  1. Click the register button at the top of this screen and create a new account
  2. Login and start matching with people using our HobbyMatch search tool
  3. Use our chatroom for general discussions and private messages to arrange activities
